Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our data career program.

Do I need a degree or tech background?
No! Our program is specifically designed for career changers with no technical background. We start from the basics and build up your skills systematically. Many of our most successful graduates came from teaching, retail, hospitality, and other non-tech fields.
How long does it take to complete?
Most students complete the program in 4-6 months while working full-time. The curriculum is self-paced, so you can move faster or slower based on your schedule and learning pace.
What kind of support do I get?
You get unlimited access to our coaching team, weekly live Q&A sessions, a private community of 80,000+ members, resume reviews, interview prep, and personalized career guidance. We're with you every step of the way.
Will I get real work experience?
Yes! Our program includes a real internship where you'll work on actual data projects. This gives you legitimate experience to put on your resume and talk about in interviews.
What if I can't find a job after completing?
We provide lifetime support. If you don't land a role right away, we'll continue working with you on your resume, interview skills, and job search strategy until you do. Our graduates have a 94% placement rate within 6 months.
